## Overview

This page documents the tasks, progress, and learning outcomes related to the Mechanical track of the robotics summer training program.

The Mechanical track focuses on understanding robot structure, mechanical design, mechanisms, materials, manufacturing methods, and the physical components used in robotics systems.
